How Auto Blogging Works

  1. Generate theme-aligned topic ideas for AI + PM + history tracks.
  2. Run SERP research once for each selected topic cluster.
  3. Create multiple SEO-friendly titles and keyword maps from that cluster.
  4. Generate long-form articles with FAQ and actionable PM implications.
  5. Auto-publish to the blog, with optional distribution to Dev.to.
